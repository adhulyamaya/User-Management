Project Description:
------------------------

Account Management System for a company, focusing on financial transactions related to sales and purchases between the company, customers, and vendors.

Main Admin: Responsible for the company and managing secondary admins (staff).
----------
Staff (Secondary Admin): Responsible for managing customers and vendors, entering sales/purchase details.
-----------------------
Customers: Track customer transactions, like sales, amounts paid, etc. but no login system.
---------
Vendors: Track vendor transactions, like purchases, amounts due, etc. but no login system.
-------
Sales and Purchases: Tracks the financial transactions for customers and vendors.
-------------------
